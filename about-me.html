Ko Noah Smith ahau

I’m passionate about innovation — not just futurism, but also the ingenuity of the past: lost taonga, ancestral knowledge, and the deep connections between history, the present, and what’s yet to come.

To me, design is a tool for progress. It’s how we imagine new futures, build better interfaces with technology, and move visual storytelling forward.

I’m guided by the wisdom of my ancestors — by how they observed the world, connected with nature, and found inspiration in both the seen and unseen. Their principles continue to shape how I approach design today.

My work is grounded in curiosity: a respect for the unknown, a love of learning, and a desire to stay open to the pursuit of knowledge in an ever-evolving world.
